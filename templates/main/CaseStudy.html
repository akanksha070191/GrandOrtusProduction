Implementing a Zero Trust Network at Healthcare Institute
  • Problems:

    Healthcare sectors are highly susceptibile to cyber threats like Protection of Sensitive Data, Complex Access Management, Compliance and Regulatory Requirements, Vulnerabilities from IoT and Medical Devices.

  • Solutions:

    We worked on Zero Trust Network Implementation, which was achieved by following steps 1.Identity and Access Management (IAM), 2.Network Access Control (NAC), 3.Secure SD-WAN and Gateway-Level Security, 4.Server Load Balancing and Web Application Firewall (WAF), 5.Endpoint and Server Security.

  • Tool:

    Arcon PIM/PAM and Identity Management (IM) for robust control, Aruba ClearPass to manage device access, Fortinet Firewalls were implemented as part of an SD-WAN, SentinelOne for comprehensive endpoint protection.

  • Time Line:

    90 Days
