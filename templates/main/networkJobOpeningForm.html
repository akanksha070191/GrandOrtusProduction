Job Description

Technical Skills:-

  • Network Design & Implementation: Ability to design, install, and manage network systems (LAN, WAN, WLAN, VPN).
  • Network Hardware Expertise: Experience with routers, switches, firewalls, and other networking devices (e.g., Cisco, Juniper, Fortinet).
  • Troubleshooting: Proficiency in diagnosing and resolving network performance issues.
  • Protocols: Knowledge of networking protocols such as TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, BGP, OSPF, and MPLS.
  • Security: Understanding of network security concepts, including firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems, and VPNs.
  • Tools: Familiarity with network monitoring tools (e.g., Wireshark, SolarWinds, Nagios).

Soft Skills:-

  • Communication: Ability to communicate technical information to non-technical stakeholders.
  • Team Collaboration: Work effectively with IT teams and external vendors.
  • Time Management: Handle multiple projects and deadlines efficiently.

Optional but Desirable Skills:-

  • Flexibility to work during off-hours or weekends if required for troubleshooting or system upgrades.
  • Familiarity with ITIL processes and frameworks.

Job Category: Network Engineer

Job Type: Full Time

Job Location: Remote
